Exterior

Exterior styling on the 2014 BMW M5 include a new ‘M kidney grille’, which features the model designation with slats that mimic the new 20 inch double-spoke design alloy wheels. Like the previous version xenon headlights are standard on the M5, there is now the option of Adaptive LED Headlights. The tail lamps, like the rest of the 5 Series family, also gets tweaked a little. The exhaust tips are now finished in black chrome.

Interior

The 12.3-inch touchscreen display in the top center of the well-designed dashboard houses the standard BMW's Live Cockpit Professional, which includes another 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster. The infotainment screen also comes with one of the best infotainment systems in the business, called the iDrive 7.0, which comes standard with Bluetooth connectivity, HD Radio, SiriusXM, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto. Another noteworthy feature is the 16-speaker Harman Kardon system, which gives you a good listening experience. But for $3,400 more, you may upgrade to a 16-speaker Bowers & Wilkins Diamond surround-sound system.
